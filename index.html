Are you feeling bored?

What does it feel like to be bored?

Boredom is marked by an empty feeling, as well as a sense of frustration with that emptiness. When you are bored, you may have a limited attention span and lack of interest in what is happening around you.
You may feel apathetic, fatigued, nervous, or jittery.

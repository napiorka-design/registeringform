What does it feel like to be bored?

Boredom is marked by an empty feeling, as well as a sense of frustration with that emptiness. When you are bored, you may have a limited attention span and lack of interest in what is happening around you.
You may feel apathetic, fatigued, nervous, or jittery.

Boredom is characterized by a feeling of emptiness and frustration with the lack of stimulation. This can result in limited attention span, disinterest in the surroundings and other emotional symptoms such as apathy, fatigue, nervousness, or jitters. Additionally, boredom can have a significant impact on mental health and can lead to decreased motivation, creativity, and productivity. To avoid the negative effects of boredom, it's important to engage in activities that bring joy and fulfillment.
